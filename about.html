About This App

The BVSD Classroom Scheduler is designed to make scheduling during office hours easier and more efficient for both staff and students. Using your BVSD Google account, you can log in to see or manage your assignments.

For staff: You can assign students to your room, request specific students for extra help, and request tutors based on academic needs. All scheduling is processed on a first-come, first-served basis.

For students: You can see where you’re assigned during office hours, view available tutoring opportunities, and sign up if you meet the requirements.

Our goal is to simplify communication and make sure everyone is where they need to be — without the paperwork.
